About Us

WE WILL WIN TOGETHER – THAT’S OUR PROMISE.

Al Yamin, a company started and promoted by two young enterprising trade professionals in 2014, with more than decade experience in international trade. Al Yamin is now one of the most reputed and trusted Consulting, Manufacturing and Trading Company, of Halaal Buffalo Meat from India to the world. India is one of the largest producing countries of agricultural commodities and World’s largest exporter of Buffalo Meat, with annual exports of over 2.2 Million Tones CWE. Annual exports of over USD 4 Billion. We have 70 world class Government of India (APEDA) approved Integrated Buffalo Meat Factories exporting to over more than 80 countries.

Our team members are skilled in understanding and growing your business. We use our experience and skills in maximizing your profits. Different regions have different norms and product specifications. Al Yamin maintains partner service list of over 100 companies and consultants who we match with specific client needs.
