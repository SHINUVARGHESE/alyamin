Scope of Expertise

Al Yamin is available to help you strategize and launch your meat-based projects, whether a wholesale importer and distributor, whether a butcher shop, traditional processing plant, restaurant, or on-farm slaughter facility. Our expertise falls within the scope of red meat processing, including:

Procurement of best raw material.

Certified organic.

Government approved abattoirs.

Packing, branding, labelling for wholesale and retail.

Humane handling including Halaal, Animal Welfare Approved certified raising & slaughtering.

Our belief is that everyone deserves high quality and responsibly raised meat - with a fair price for everyone involved.
